One-pan Thai coconut chicken and rice

Description

This one-pan Thai coconut chicken and rice is a flavorful, fuss-free meal that brings vibrant Southeast Asian flavors to your kitchen with minimal cleanup. Tender chicken thighs are simmered in creamy coconut milk with fragrant garlic, ginger, and Thai red curry paste, infusing the rice with rich, aromatic taste.

With everything cooked in a single skillet, the rice soaks up the savory-sweet sauce while staying fluffy and satisfying. Garnished with fresh cilantro and a squeeze of lime, it’s a complete, comforting dish that’s perfect for weeknight dinners or easy entertaining.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 chicken breasts, thickly slices on an angle
  • (1/2) 450g pkt Thai green curry sauce (cook-in)
  • 2x 250g packets 90-second microwave coconut rice
  • 450g packet frozen steam fresh bean, broccoli, sugar snap peas
  • 1/2 cup fresh coriander sprigs

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large, deep frying pan over high heat. Add chicken. Cook, turning for 2 to 3 minutes or until browned. Add 1/2 cup curry sauce. Cook for 3 to 4 minutes or until sauce thickens and chicken is cooked through. Transfer to a plate.
  2. Place remaining sauce in a small microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on HIGH (100%) for 20 to 30 seconds or until heated through.
  3. Meanwhile, add rice and vegetables to pan. Cook, stirring, for 2 to 3 minutes or until vegetables and rice are heated through. Top with chicken and drizzle with remaining sauce. Serve sprinkled with coriander.
